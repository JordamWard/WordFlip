This policy explains what WordFlip ("we", "us") collects, why, and your choices. By using WordFlip you agree to this policy.

Who we are

WordFlip is a word game operated by [YOUR NAME OR COMPANY]. Contact: [YOUR CONTACT EMAIL].

What we collect

  • Account info — if you create an account: your email address, a password (stored securely hashed, never in plain text), and a chosen username / display name.
  • Gameplay data — scores, streaks, daily results, achievements, coin balance, and purchases, so your progress follows your account.
  • Purchase records — if you buy coins, we record that a purchase happened and how many coins to grant. We never see or store your card details — payments are handled by our processor (Stripe).
  • On-device storage — we keep some data in your browser's local storage (e.g. cached balance, settings, tutorial-seen flag) so the app loads fast and works offline.
  • Basic analytics — privacy-friendly, aggregate usage stats via Plausible, which does not use cookies or track you across sites.

You can play the Daily Challenge and solo games without an account; in that case your progress stays only on your device.

What we do NOT collect

We do not sell your data. We do not run third-party advertising or ad-tracking. We do not collect your contacts, location, or device identifiers for tracking.

Service providers we use

  • Supabase — accounts, database, and game data hosting.
  • Netlify — website/app hosting.
  • Stripe — payment processing (they handle card data under their own privacy policy).
  • Plausible — cookieless, privacy-friendly analytics.

Children

WordFlip is not directed to children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from them. If you believe a child has provided us information, contact us and we will delete it.

Your rights & choices

  • Access, correct, or delete your account data — email [YOUR CONTACT EMAIL] and we will help.
  • Delete your account entirely, which removes your account data from our systems.
  • Depending on where you live (e.g. EEA/UK, California), you may have additional rights to access, port, or restrict processing of your data.

Data retention & security

We keep account and gameplay data while your account exists, and delete it on request. We use industry-standard measures (encryption in transit, hashed passwords) to protect your data, though no method is 100% secure.

Changes

We may update this policy; we'll change the "last updated" date above and, for significant changes, notify you in the app.
